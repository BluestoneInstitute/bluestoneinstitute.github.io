Bluestone Institute

Bluestone Institute, founded in 2021, is a not-for-profit* research institute and think tank. We encourage the investigation, promotion, dissemination, and application of the social sciences and social science research – in particular, the discipline of economics – for the common good. Through the work of our Research Fellows and with the support of Research Interns we strive to discover and share new insights while staying true to our values of Independence, Integrity, Creativity, and Objectivity.

*Approval as an IRS 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation is pending.
